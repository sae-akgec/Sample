Effi-car

The EFFI-CARs is a unique platform for engineering students to put their learning into practical purposes. The event is aimed at designing and fabricating a 2 seater, 4-wheeled, electric powered vehicles. The vehicle should be able to negotiate the day-to-day city road conditions while keeping the passengersâ€™ and pedestrians safe. The objective of the event is to promote environmental sustainability by creating innovative mobility solutions. The teams of 7-10 engineering students are tasked with fabricating the economic electrical vehicles with low fabricating, maintenance and running cost. The event will gauge the capability of vehicles on various commercial and design aspects through a series of static and dynamic tests.
